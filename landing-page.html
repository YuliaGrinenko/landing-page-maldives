Maldives

One of the most serene, romantic, and wildly scenic destinations in the world

Beaches, lagoons, islands, the places to visit in Maldives can lend you a diverse and most enthralling experience on your holiday. With the splendor of nature and the most charismatic vibes, the island destination in South Asia is the best holiday destination for every vacationer, honeymooner, or traveler. Alluring people with its enchanting beauty of blue and white shores, Maldives is a real paradise that offers every luxury and thrill. Discover the excellence of those exotic water villas or relish the taste of authentic food while you are on a holiday in this land of boundless beauty.

Things to do in the Maldives

Staying IN AN OVER WATER VILLA

Picture the Maldives, and these are probably the first thing that spring to mind. And while there’s a lot more to holidays here than sitting back and sunbathing, these distinctive rooms are primed for topping up your tan, thanks to a private deck which juts out over the water.

Snorkling

The Maldives are one of the best places in the world to go snorkelling. There are over 1,000 coral reefs, which are home to even more species of marine life. And if you’re lucky, you might get to swim alongside the likes of dolphins, manta rays and turtles – so it’s a paradise for nature lovers.

Scuba diving

VISIT AN UNINHABITED ISLAND

There 1,000 of desewerted islands in the Maldives. As they’re all tucked away in the immense stretch of the Indian Ocean, they are some of the most pristine retreats on the globe. If you opt for a day trip, you can spend the day swimming in one of the lagoons, explore the shoreline by foot, or simply roll out your beach towel and take it all in.

SURF IT UP

Thanks to its tropical climate and northerly position in the Indian Ocean, the Maldives is a heaven on earth for surfers. You can catch the very best waves from April to October, when they total around three to eight feet in height.
